Synopsis

On July 12, 2025, Maple & Finch Hospitality Ltd., a U.S.-based restaurant chain, reported a major cyber intrusion traced to the United Kingdom. The suspect is alleged to have accessed servers in Boston, Massachusetts by exploiting outdated security protocols.

The attack disrupted online orders, erased bookings, and corrupted payment records, forcing multiple restaurant closures. Damages are estimated at over $480,000 USD.

Digital forensics indicate the suspect operated alone, concealing activity through proxy networks. Despite no evidence of financial gain, the scale of disruption has classified the incident as a serious cybercrime.
